# Argento Icons Bar

![shopify theme general settings](/images/shopify/icons-bar.png)

The theme looks great with informative icons: **FREE SHIPPING, 24/7 SUPPORT, EXTENDED RETURN PERIOD, CHOOSE DELIVERY DAY**.  All icons are organized under a header.

![shopify theme general settings](/images/shopify/icons-settings.png)

You can enable/disable the icon. When you hover over the icon, you will see the visibility badge.

Please click the icon to start customizing.

 1. You can specify the text for the icon.
 2. You can choose up to 4 icons in the drop-down, or set the value to None.
 3. You can add a link to other pages of your Shopify site.
